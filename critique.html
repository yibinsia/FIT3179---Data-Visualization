Global GDP Overview

Global GDP (2023)

This map displays the distribution of Gross Domestic Product (GDP) across countries for the year 2023. The GDP values are shown in billions of US dollars. Darker shades indicate higher GDP values, while lighter shades represent lower GDP. Clicking on any country reveals more detailed information about its GDP.

Life Expectancy vs GDP (2023)

The scatter plot visualizes the relationship between GDP and life expectancy for different countries in 2023. The size of each bubble represents the population of the country, and the color indicates the region. You can use the filters below the plot to explore specific regions or GDP ranges.

Top 10 GDP Countries in Recent Years

This line chart shows the GDP trends of the top 10 countries over recent years, allowing users to observe the growth patterns over time. Use the interactive brush to select a specific time range.

GDP Distribution by Region (2023)

This radial chart represents the distribution of GDP among different regions in 2023. The segment size indicates the GDP share for each region, helping visualize the relative economic contributions across continents.
